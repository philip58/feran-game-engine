This is an in space flying and avoiding asteroids game. 
You move with WASD W for up, A for left, S for down and D for right. 
You can also shoot laser projectiles with the left, right, up and down arrow keys.
Asteroids are falling down and you have to avoid them. 
If you get hit you catch fire. Once they all fall down and you reach the end you will be safe and have won.

Unique features: 
Fast/smooth movement with up down left right and diagonal movement.
Shooting projectiles that move independently.
Randomization of moving obstacles that the player must avoid.
